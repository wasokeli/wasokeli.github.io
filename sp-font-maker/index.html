SP Font Maker!

make your own sitelen pona font, just by drawing on some paper. here are some examples!!

  • nasin sitelen musi tan nasa [tomo.pona.]
  • sitelen [meli awen jan e ko ale] pi alasa wan taso
  • sitelen pona pi jan [walo.telo.sama.]
  • sitelen [kiwen en telo en sijelo insa] nasa
  • sitelen luka [kon insa lape open]
  • sitelen linja tan jan [nena open pi uta]
  • sitelen meli kule tan meli Siliwa
  • linja open tan Keli [kasi esun lete ilo]
  • sitelen soweli linja tan soweli [seli.lukin.pali utala]
  • sitelen pi linja telo tan soweli [seli.lukin.pali utala]
  • sitelen luka tan jan [ijo wile ijo]
  • jan [sama olin namako jaki ala] li sitelen e pu kepeken wawa mute.

fill out this image*, and send it to someone who's managed to install the script. they'll give you your font file:

there are pixel templates too. upscale as needed: 2px, 4px, 6px, 8px, 10px, 12px, 16px

*if you prefer real paper, you can print the PDF or the SVG, write on it with pen, and scan it with the free adobe scan app! you can also just use something like mspaint or krita or gimp.

installation instructions are here, but they're pretty scary if you don't know the command line. you can ask for help on the font server, or in the thread on the big server.
